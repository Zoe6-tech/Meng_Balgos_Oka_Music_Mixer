How To Play Mu-Sion Game

Music Fusion from China, Philippines and Japan

You are about to hear some of the traditional instruments derived from Asian countries such as China, the Philippines, and Japan. The goal of this game is to introduce to the younger generation that few indigenous instruments exist before all the modernity takes place.

CHINA:
Dizi
Guzheng
Pipa
Erhu
PHILPPINES:
Kulintang
Luntang
Diwas
Gambal
JAPAN:
Koto
Shamisen
Shinobue
Kozutsumi

GAME MECHANICS:

Each doll will have a designated instrument to drag on and hear the music.
  • Step 1. Read this step by step instruction.
  • Step 2. Choose a doll type to start to play.
  • Step 3. Drag an instrument which you feel interested and drop on the doll.
  • Step 4. You may click the “Pause” button on the upper portion of the application to pause the sound.
  • Step 5. You may click the “restart” button on the upper portion of the application to restart the sound.

    • Hope you had fun and new learning too!
